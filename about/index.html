02 — PROBLEMS & SOLUTIONS

REAL PROBLEMS,
REAL FIXES

Every feature in Pocket Money Tracker was designed to directly solve a real frustration young savers face.

😤
Problem
I don't know where my money goes each month. I just check my balance and it's already low.
→
✅
Solution
The Transaction Tracker logs every spend with category, date, and notes. The dashboard doughnut chart shows exactly where money is going by category — instantly.
😩
Problem
I set a savings goal but have no idea if I'm on track or how long it will take to reach it.
→
✅
Solution
The Goals Module shows an animated progress bar, percentage achieved, days until deadline, and total remaining — so you always know where you stand.
😵
Problem
My pocket money is in AED but I want to compare prices in USD, GBP, or send money abroad — I have to Google every conversion manually.
→
✅
Solution
The Currency Converter fetches live rates for 18+ currencies. The multi-grid view shows your amount converted into all currencies simultaneously.
📱
Problem
Other finance apps are too complex, require accounts, or cost money — just to track basic daily spending.
→
✅
Solution
Pocket Money Tracker is completely free, requires no sign-up, runs in any browser, and saves data locally. Zero friction, zero cost.
🌞
Problem
I use my phone in bright environments and dark-only apps hurt my eyes. I want a choice of theme.
→
✅
Solution
One-click Dark / Light Mode toggle with full interface retheme. Preference is saved in localStorage so it persists across sessions automatically.
🧮
Problem
I need to calculate how much I'll save over time with interest, or split a bill — I keep switching between apps.
→
✅
Solution
The Tools suite includes an advanced calculator with memory, compound interest calculator, and random number generator — all in one place, no tab-switching needed.

04 — TOOLS SUITE

BUILT-IN
POWER TOOLS

Stop switching apps. Every tool a student or saver needs, baked right in.

🧮
Advanced Calculator
Full-featured calculator with expression history, brackets, percentage, square root, and memory functions.
  • MS / MR / MC memory slots
  • Expression display (shows full equation)
  • √x and x² functions
  • Percentage support
  • Bracket grouping
📈
Compound Interest
See how your money grows over time. Enter principal, rate, and years — get final value, interest earned, and growth percentage instantly.
  • Real-time calculation on input
  • Final value display
  • Interest earned
  • % growth indicator
EXAMPLE: AED 1000 @ 5% for 5 years
AED 1,276.28
+276.28 earned (+27.6%)
🎲
RNG + Quick Notes
Random number generator with custom min/max range. Plus a persistent sticky-note system for reminders, spending limits, and ideas.
  • Custom min / max range
  • Large bold result display
  • Timestamped notes
  • Delete individual or clear all
  • Saved in localStorage
Bonus Feature
📤 One-Click CSV Export
Export your entire transaction history as a CSV file with one click. Open it in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app for deeper analysis.
